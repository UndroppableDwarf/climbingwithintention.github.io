Alyssa Meyer

Alyssa Meyer is a highly accomplished climber and certified fitness professional with over a decade of climbing experience and seven years of coaching.

Alyssa has competed internationally in lead, boulder, and speed—representing South Africa in multiple World Cups and Youth World Championships, and ranking as the 3rd-fastest woman in Africa in speed climbing (2023).

With a degree in Sport & Exercise and multiple fitness qualifications, Alyssa coaches climbers of all ages and levels. She specializes in helping athletes build confidence, break mental and physical barriers, and prepare effectively for competitions.

Rates: Private Climbing Coaching or Conditioning = R500/h, Semi-Private (2 - 3 people) coaching or conditioning = R400 per person per session. Larger and more specific session prices can be found in the booking page.
